# AutoStream — Product Knowledge Base

## About AutoStream

AutoStream is a cloud-based video editing platform purpose-built for content creators,
social media managers, and lean marketing teams. We pair a professional-grade editing
timeline with AI-powered automation — auto-captions, smart aspect-ratio resizing,
one-click color grading, and batch export — so you can go from raw footage to
published video in minutes instead of hours. Over 50,000 creators across YouTube,
TikTok, and Instagram trust AutoStream to power their daily content workflow.

## Company Policies

### Billing and Refunds

- All plans are billed on a **monthly recurring** basis. Your billing date is the
  calendar day you originally subscribed.
- You may cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation takes effect at the
  end of the current billing cycle — you retain access until then.
- **Full refunds are available within 7 days of any charge.** After 7 days, no
  refunds are issued for that billing period. No exceptions.
- If you downgrade from Pro to Basic mid-cycle, the change takes effect at the
  start of your next billing cycle. You keep Pro features until then.
- If you upgrade from Basic to Pro mid-cycle, the upgrade is effective immediately.
  You are charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the current cycle.

### Support

- **Basic plan** customers have access to the AutoStream Community Forum. Our
  community team monitors the forum and typically responds within 48 business hours.
- **Pro plan** customers receive **24/7 priority support** via live chat and email.
  Average first-response time is under 2 hours.
- All customers can access our self-service Help Center at help.autostream.io,
  which includes 200+ articles, video tutorials, and troubleshooting guides.
- Bug reports and feature requests can be submitted by any customer through the
  Help Center or by emailing support@autostream.io.

### Account and Data

- Your project files and exported videos are stored securely on AWS-backed cloud
  infrastructure with AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit.
- If your account is inactive for more than **12 consecutive months** with no active
  subscription, we reserve the right to delete stored project files after sending a
  30-day advance notice to your registered email.
- You can export and download all your data at any time from the Account Settings
  page. AutoStream does not claim ownership of any content you create.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. Is there a free trial?

Yes! AutoStream offers a **7-day free trial of the Pro plan** for all new users. No
credit card is required to start. At the end of the trial, you can choose to
subscribe to Basic or Pro. If you don't choose a plan, your account remains active
in a read-only state — you can view and download existing projects but cannot create
new exports.

### 2. Can I switch between plans at any time?

Absolutely. You can upgrade from Basic to Pro at any time — the change is immediate
and you're charged a prorated amount for the rest of the cycle. If you downgrade
from Pro to Basic, the switch takes effect at the start of your next billing cycle so
you don't lose access to Pro features you've already paid for.

### 3. What video file formats does AutoStream support?

**Import:** MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, WEBM, and ProRes. Maximum upload file size is 5 GB
per video on Basic and 20 GB per video on Pro.
**Export:** MP4 (H.264 and H.265), MOV, and GIF. You can select resolution, frame
rate (up to 60 fps on Pro), and quality preset during export.

### 4. Do AI captions support multiple languages?

Yes — AutoStream AI captions currently support **12 languages**: English, Spanish,
French, German, Portuguese, Italian, Dutch, Hindi, Japanese, Korean, Mandarin
Chinese, and Arabic. Caption accuracy is 98%+ for English and 95%+ for other
supported languages. Captions can be burned into the video or exported as a separate
SRT/VTT file.

### 5. Can I collaborate with my team on AutoStream?

Team collaboration is available on the **Pro plan**. You can invite up to 5 team
members to a shared workspace. Each member gets their own login and can edit
projects, leave comments on the timeline, and export videos. All team members share
the workspace's 100 GB storage quota. Activity history is tracked so you can see who
made which changes and when.

### 6. What happens if I exceed my 10-video export limit on the Basic plan?

Once you've used all 10 exports for the month, you'll see a prompt to upgrade to
Pro for unlimited exports. You **cannot** purchase additional one-off exports on the
Basic plan. Your export count resets on your monthly billing date. Drafts and
in-progress edits are not affected — you can continue editing, you just can't export
until the count resets or you upgrade.

### 7. Does AutoStream add a watermark to exported videos?

No. AutoStream **never** adds watermarks to any exports on any plan, including during
the free trial. What you export is fully yours.
